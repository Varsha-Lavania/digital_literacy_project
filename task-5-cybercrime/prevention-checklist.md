# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Do not click on suspicious links in SMS, emails, or WhatsApp messages  
- Always verify unknown callers claiming to be bank officials or support agents  
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
- Install apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store  
- Avoid sharing personal information on public platforms  

## UPI & Financial Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone  
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter a PIN to receive money  
- Do not approve unknown “collect requests” in UPI apps  
- Avoid scanning QR codes sent by strangers to receive payments  
- Double-check UPI ID and name before sending money  

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930**
