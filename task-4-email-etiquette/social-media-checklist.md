# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s
- Think before posting anything online  
- Maintain a respectful and positive tone  
- Protect your personal information and privacy  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Use social media for learning and networking  

## Don’ts
- Do not share fake news or misinformation  
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Do not overshare personal details  
- Avoid engaging in online arguments or trolling  
- Do not use others' content without permission
